Mabel



The tabby with a mouth





Mable loves a fuss but is very undemanding. She will play with her toys alone, or will bat a ball back to you. Equally she is happy to snooze in a nice warm bed. Fate has been unkind to Mable and she has what we believe is a food allergy which makes her skin extremely itchy. As you will see from her pictures she is now very well and stable on her current diet and medication.We feel she needs an indoor home only to ensure control of her diet and reduce possible exposure to other allergens such as fleas. A new owner will need to be very strict about managing Purdy's treatment and we may be able to fund the ongoing medication costs. Pretty Mable has been through a lot, and if you feel you have the experience to give her the care and love she needs she will reward you with lots of purrs.
