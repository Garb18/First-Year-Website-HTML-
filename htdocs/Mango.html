Mango



The ginger sweetheart





This handsome boy is looking for a home where he can have lots of fuss and space to go exploring. Mango is friendly and loves attention, but can forget his manners and give you a little nip. He has been stray for a while and would clearly like to be able to go outside again. He needs a patient owner with some cat experience who is prepared to give him a chance to settle into a forever home. Click here to see a video of Mango fascinated by a squirrel!
