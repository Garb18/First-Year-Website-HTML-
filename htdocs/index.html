Welcome to the Shrub Hill Cattery Website!



Here at Shrub Hill Cattery, our number one priority is finding loving homes for our cats. Most of the year we deal with rescues and breeding Maine Coons. However, around Christmas our priority shifts to finding loving homes for our rescues who have been with us since the start of the year.

We feel it's very important to share with you an impression of the way our cats live - and the way we live with them - so please take your time and peruse our website as you will find many different photos and videos that reveal their way of living. We do NOT have outside buildings for our cats either; they are part of our family and therefor live with us, within our home.

We value your communication with ourselves, and have taken on feedback from local residents and have helped structure a reliable and trustworthy cattery. But don't take our word for it, see what our previous clients have said about our cattery, and the beautiful animals they end up taking homes!

We got our Tizzy from Shrub Hill Cattery, and she settled in perfectly. Benedict and the team gave us so much advice specific to Tizzy it was too much to take in! We got a lovely little brochure with information filled specifically about Tizzy. We knew what she like to eat, what she liked playing with, and her favourite spots to sunbathe!
