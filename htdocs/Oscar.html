Oscar



One Moggy to rule them all





Due to unforeseen circumstances the owner of Oscar can not longer keep him. So, Tommy is looking for a new loving home. Oscar is a little cat that is independent and loves being around people. He likes sitting in the sun but also likes to be inside on somebodies lap. He likes playing especially with his toy mouse. As he likes being around people all the time he would like to find a new home where somebody is at home most of the time in a quiet environment. Ideally a farm or home that has a lot of land as he is used to exploring on the farm he lives at now.
